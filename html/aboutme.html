Education:

I received my 2 year degree from Tompkins Cortland Community College in 2005 and a Bachelor's of Arts in History from the University of Fredonia NY in 2009.

After College:

It wasn't until after college that I realized my professional interests were strictly tied to computers. I had already spent a lot of money on an education that I probably wasn't going to use much. The one unintended silver lining to earning a degree in History was I read a lot and I practiced reading a lot. This comes in handy when learning about computers as tech books are long and dry. I got heavily in to system administration project management before doing anything with code. It wasn't until 2016 when I got to write some SQL code for the first time that I realized I enjoyed coding and I wanted to learn more about it. I've come to understand nobody is going to teach you, and you can waste a lot of time trying to find a job where you will learn on the fly. Ultimately I decided to start this blog as a vehicle I could use to teach myself things like Javascript, PHP, and whatever else I feel like reading about in the future.
